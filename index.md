## Welcome

This website contains additional experiments for the ICML 2026 submission, *Toward Calibrated Mixture-of-Experts Under Distribution Shift*. Here, we add experiments that extend the paper's evaluation to additional datasets with natural shifts. We are working under limited compute, but we hope the experiments we select demonstrate that the fragility of routing under shift holds across different datasets and setups.

If you are reading this, we thank you very much for taking the time to look over these additional experiments.
